Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

What is Pippo?
Pippo is a discord bot that has games focused around Bollywood and traditional Indian classroom games.
How do I add Pippo to my discord server?
You can add Pippo to your discord server by visiting the website and clicking on the 'Add to Discord' button.
What are some of the games available on Pippo?
Some of the games available on Pippo include Antakshari, Dumb Charades, and Bollywood Trivia.
Can I suggest new games to be added to Pippo?
Yes, you can suggest new games to be added to Pippo by joining the support server and submitting your suggestions.
Is Pippo free to use?
Yes, Pippo is completely free to use.
